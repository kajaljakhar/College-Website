MISSION

The institution aims to make the students aware of Indian culture through awareness programmes, to empower the women through active participation in various activities with dignity and confidence, preserving the social ethos, Vedic doctrines and our cultural heritage, to prepare and enable the students to face the challenges of life by making them conscious of their rights.

VISION

The institution strongly believes in imparting quality education to the girl students and inculcates qualities of leadership, competence, excellence and self confidence to enable them to regenerate the society which has posed challenges for them. Our institution sincerely believes in building multi-dimensional personality of the students having strong faith in the philosophy of Arya Samaj combined with current trends of education.

OBJECTIVE

The main objective of the institution is to cater to the needs of the girl students of deprived and depressed classes especially belonging to rural and border areas where scheduled castes, scheduled tribes and backward castes reside in large number. Bringing such students in arena of education and making them self dependent is of utmost concern. To give them value-based education so that they can play a significant role in nation-building is the major objective of the institution. Efforts are done to explore the potential and latent talent of the students by providing them a congenial environment in order to develop their overall personality.

FROM THE DESK OF PRINCIPAL







It is an honour and pleasure to serve D.A.V. fraternity as a Principal and of an institution like Gopichand Arya Mahila College known for its academic excellence and accredited by NAAC as ‘A’ Grade College. Established in 1972, the institute is dedicated to impart higher education to young women which is very noble, potent and conscientious task.


I strongly believe that investing in women by imparting them higher education can unlock their potential on a transformational scale. We provide opportunities to our students so that they may contribute to the best of their abilities and prove a great asset. We are fortunate to have talented, highly committed and dynamic faculty and support staff to ensure the best learning environment. We are proud to have shining Alumni and philanthropic donors who are always ready to care and nourish the institute with zeal and enthusiasm.


Marching ahead towards Golden Jubilee with proud legacy of 45 years, the institute has excelled in every field. A number of our students have registered their presence in the University Merit list and others have brought laurels in sports and cultural activities. We ensure to equip our students with knowledge and values leading to holistic growth by providing them overall facilities for student progression. I look forward to work with all of you to attain new heights.



Dr. Rekha Sood Handa


Principal
